FEATURES

Everything you need
to control your PC

Full-featured remote control over your local Wi-Fi. No accounts, no cloud, no latency.

🖱️
Touchpad & Mouse

Use your phone screen as a precision touchpad. 2-finger scroll, tap click, long-press right-click. Adjustable sensitivity and FPS cap up to 144Hz.

⌨️
Full Keyboard

Complete on-screen keyboard with modifier keys (Ctrl, Alt, Shift, Win), function keys F1–F12, and all special keys. Landscape mode shows keyboard + touchpad together.

🎵
Media & Volume

Control playback, skip tracks, adjust volume with a real slider. Media keys work with Spotify, YouTube, VLC — anything playing on your PC.

⚡
Quick Actions

12 instant action buttons — Screenshot, Task Manager, Lock Screen, Show Desktop, Run Dialog, Clipboard Paste, Window Snap, and more.

🔴
Macro Recorder

Record a sequence of commands and replay them with one tap. Perfect for repetitive tasks or custom workflows. Save and run macros on demand.

📡
Auto-Discovery

No IP address typing needed. Three simultaneous discovery methods — UDP broadcast, LocalSend multicast, TCP subnet scan — find your PC in seconds.

HOW IT WORKS

Up and running
in 60 seconds

1
Install on PC

Download and run the Controllax .exe on your Windows PC. No installation wizard — just run it.

2
Start Server

Click ▶ Start Server in the sidebar. The app broadcasts on your local network automatically.

3
Open App

Open Controllax on your Android phone. Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network.

4
Auto-Connect

Tap Auto-Discover. Your PC appears instantly. Tap connect — you're in control.
